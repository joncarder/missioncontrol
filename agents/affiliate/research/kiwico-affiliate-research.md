## Commission Structure

### Content Sites
- **Renewing subscriptions:** Up to $20 per sale
- **Non-renewing subscriptions:** Up to $10 per order
- **Store orders:** 10% commission

### Coupon/Deal Sites
- **Subscriptions:** Up to $2 per order
- **Store orders:** 2% commission

### Cookie Duration
- 30 days

## Program Perks
- Access to KiwiCo Store product feed
- Special discounts and seasonal promotional opportunities
- Dedicated Account Management Team
- Creative assets available (logos, photos, videos, one-pagers)
