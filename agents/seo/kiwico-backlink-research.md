## KiwiCo Backlink Profile Analysis

### Affiliate Program Intelligence
**Source:** https://www.kiwico.com/affiliate-program

**Commission Structure:**
- Content sites: Up to $20 for renewing subscription sales, up to $10 for non-renewing
- Coupon sites: Up to $2 per subscription order
- Store orders: 10% commission (2% for coupon sites)
- Cookie duration: 30 days
- Network: CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)

**Key Insight:** My Lil Startup can differentiate by offering 16% commission vs KiwiCo's 8-10% structure, plus the value prop of "real entrepreneurship" vs just STEM learning.

Based on industry analysis and competitive research:

**Domain Authority:** Estimated DA 60-70 (high authority in kids/STEM niche)

**Top Link Categories for KiwiCo:**
1. **Parenting Blogs** - Gift guides, educational toy reviews, subscription box roundups

3. **Deal/Coupon Sites** - RetailMeNot, Honey, coupon blogs
4. **Kids Activity Blogs** - Craft blogs, activity roundups, educational content
5. **Resource Pages** - "Best subscription boxes for kids", "STEM gift ideas"
6. **Press Mentions** - Forbes, Parents Magazine, Good Housekeeping

**Link Patterns Observed:**
- Heavy presence on gift guide articles (holiday seasons)
